Design Direction Notes

  • All concepts follow F1 multi-zone logo placement: chest (primary), sleeve (secondary), back (impact)
  • Brand palette: #0A0A0A black body, #BB8F52 gold accents, white logo/text
  • Recommended real-world execution: embroidered chest logos, sublimated sleeve panels
  • Option A (Coverall) = maximum motorsport authenticity — ideal for outdoor/heavy detailing
  • Option B (Polo+Pants) = client-facing professionalism — best for initial vehicle walk-around
  • Option C (Jacket) = brand statement — wear arriving/departing client property
  • Ideal system: Bomber jacket for arrival → Coverall for work → Polo for handover
