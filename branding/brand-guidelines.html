Introduction

Coastal Finest Detailing is not the cheapest — it is the finest. A premium mobile auto detailing service built on craftsmanship, precision, and respect for the vehicles we care for. This manual defines how our identity must appear in every context, without exception.

A premium mobile detailing service serving discerning clients across the Gulf Coast of Alabama. We bring the finest care directly to you — on your schedule, at your location.

III

Confident. Precise. Premium. We are bold without being flashy, exclusive without being inaccessible. Performance-driven, coast-inspired, uncompromising in quality.

Identity

Logo System

Two distinct marks form our complete identity system. Each serves a specific purpose and must never be used interchangeably. Both are proprietary — never recreate, alter, or approximate them.

Primary Mark — CFD Lettermark

The CFD lettermark is a fused monogram where C, F, and D share a continuous form united by the gold diagonal accent. Use for compact applications: uniforms, caps, stickers, profile icons, and embroidery where the full wordmark would not be legible.

Primary Version Dark — Preferred
Light BG Version Light
Special Use Version Gold — Sparingly
Secondary Mark — Full Wordmark

The full wordmark stacks the complete brand name in bold condensed italics. "FINEST" is always rendered in gold — this is non-negotiable. Use for: website header, van wrap, large format signage, marketing materials, and any context where brand recognition requires the full name.

Primary Version Dark — Preferred
Light BG Version Light
Special Use Version Gold — Events Only

Clear Space Rule

Every logo mark requires protected breathing room — a minimum clear space that must be kept free of all other graphic elements, text, and image content on all four sides.

Minimum clear space = height of the "C" letterform in the CFD mark, applied uniformly on all sides.
✓
Always Do
  • Use the dark version (on black) as the primary application across all touchpoints
  • Maintain minimum clear space around the logo at all times
  • Use SVG files for all digital and screen applications
  • Use PNG files with transparency when placed over photography
  • Apply only on the three approved background colors: black, white, gold
  • Use the CFD lettermark for compact/small-scale applications
  • Use the wordmark when communicating the full brand name is important
✗
Never Do
  • Never stretch, compress, or distort either mark in any direction
  • Never rotate the logo or apply any angle other than its original orientation
  • Never change the gold accent to any other color
  • Never recreate the logo from scratch using available fonts
  • Never place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a dark overlay
  • Never add shadows, glows, outlines, or visual effects to the logo
  • Never use either mark below 32px on screen or 0.5 inch in print

05
Applications

Brand in Use

How Coastal Finest appears across real-world touchpoints. Consistency in color, clear space, and logo version is mandatory across every application. When in doubt, reference this manual.

01
Business Card — Front
CFD lettermark top-left. Luxury vehicle photography on right with gradient overlay. Contact information bottom-left in white. 3.5" × 2" standard format. Print at 300 DPI minimum.
02
Digital Header / Banner
CFD mark top-left on dark background with vehicle photography. Works for website hero, Facebook cover, email headers. Maintain gold and white only — no additional colors.
Uniforms
CFD lettermark embroidered on chest of black polo shirts. White or gold thread only. Never use the full wordmark on uniforms — monogram only. No minimum color variations.
Van Wrap
Full wordmark on both sides. Black base with gold and white. CFD lettermark large on rear doors. Website URL and phone in Montserrat 600. No decorative elements beyond the logo system.
Digital & Social
CFD lettermark as profile icon on all platforms. Full wordmark for cover photos. Black backgrounds always. Gold used sparingly as accent — never the dominant element in compositions.

Communication

Brand Voice

How we speak is as important as how we look. Our tone is confident, precise, and premium — without arrogance. We let our work speak first. Clear language over clever language, always.

I
Confident
We state what we do without hedging or qualifying. We know our work is exceptional and our communication reflects that certainty — never arrogant, always assured.
II
Precise
Specific language. Exact pricing. Clear service descriptions. We don't use vague terms, filler words, or empty promises. Our precision in language mirrors our precision in work.
III
Local & Proud
We are Mobile, Alabama. Gulf Coast roots, premium standards. We reference our location with pride — this is not just a business, it is a commitment to our community.
